Payment Processing Issues (Venmo, PayPal, Braintree)

If you are experiencing issues with completing your order, please review the common solutions below for our supported payment methods.

üí≥ Venmo & PayPal Payment Issues

These platforms often have strict security and verification processes. If your payment is not going through, consider the following:

  • Linked Bank/Card: Ensure the bank account or credit card linked to your Venmo or PayPal account is current and has sufficient funds.
  • Account Verification: Confirm your Venmo or PayPal account is fully verified and not subject to any temporary **spending limits or holds**.
  • Browser Cache: Try **clearing your browser's cache and cookies**, or use an incognito/private browsing window, then attempt the payment again.
  • Address Mismatch: Verify that the shipping address on our site matches the address associated with your PayPal account (if applicable).
  • Note: Sometimes, large transactions may be flagged by PayPal/Venmo security. Check your email or app for a **pending authorization request**.

üîê Braintree (Credit Card) Processing Issues

Braintree handles our direct credit/debit card transactions. If your card is declined, it is usually one of these reasons:

  • Billing Address: Double-check that the billing address and zip code you entered on our checkout page exactly match the address on file with your credit card company. This is the most common issue.
  • Card Details: Ensure the **card number, expiration date, and CVV code** (the 3 or 4-digit security code) are entered correctly.
  • Card Type/Issuer: Confirm your card type (Visa, Mastercard, etc.) is supported and that your bank has not placed a **temporary hold or block** on the transaction.

‚úÖ General Troubleshooting Steps

  • Try a **different payment method** if available.
  • Attempt the transaction using a **different device** (e.g., from a mobile phone instead of a desktop).
  • If the error persists after 30 minutes, it may be a temporary system issue. Please **wait a short time and try again**.

Need More Help?
